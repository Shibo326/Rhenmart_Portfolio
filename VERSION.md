### 📱 Mobile vs Desktop

#### Mobile Optimizations
- Shorter animation durations (0.4s vs 0.6s)
- Smaller animation distances (20px vs 40px)
- No parallax effects (performance)
- No 3D transforms (performance)
- Touch-optimized interactions
- Reduced particle count
- Simplified visual effects

#### Desktop Enhancements
- Full animation durations (0.6s)
- Larger animation distances (40px)
- Parallax scrolling effects
- 3D tilt card effects
- Magnetic button interactions
- Cursor tracking and glow
- Rich hover states
- Complex visual effects
