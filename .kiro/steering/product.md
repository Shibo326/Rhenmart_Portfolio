# Product

This is the personal portfolio website of **Rhenmart Dela Cruz**, a Junior UI/UX Designer based in the Philippines.

## Purpose
A single-page portfolio showcasing design skills, services, work history, and contact information. The target audience is potential clients and employers looking to hire a UI/UX designer for freelance or full-time work.

## Sections
- **Hero** — Name, title (typing animation), social links, stats, CTA buttons
- **Services** — UI Design, UX Research, Prototyping, Design-to-Dev Collaboration
- **About** — Bio, background, download resume
- **Skills** — Core skill rings (UI Design, UX Research, Prototyping, AI-Assisted UX), tools (Figma, Framer, WIX Studio, V0), AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Loveable)
- **Portfolio** — Gallery of competitions, workshops, and seminars with filterable categories
- **Contact** — EmailJS-powered contact form + contact info cards

## Brand Identity
- Dark theme: near-black backgrounds (`#030303`, `#050505`, `#080808`)
- Primary accent: `#FF0000` (red) — used for all highlights, glows, borders, and CTAs
- Typography: Inter (sans-serif), heavy font weights for headings
- Aesthetic: Stellar/cosmic dark UI with particle fields, animated gradients, and micro-interactions
